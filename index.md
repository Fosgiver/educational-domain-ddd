## A Free Domain-Driven Design Manuscript for Educational Software Architecture

The **Educational Domain — Domain-Driven Design Manuscript** is a freely available architectural work created through an extended dialogue between human professional experience and Artificial Intelligence.

Its purpose is to explore how educational organizations can be understood before designing the information systems that support them.

This work uses **Domain-Driven Design**, **systems analysis**, **educational domain modeling**, and **ubiquitous language** to describe core concepts such as educational products, study cycles, sections, teaching sessions, assessment, financial lifecycle, operational visibility and decision support.

## What this work is

This manuscript is:

- a free architectural proposal,
- a Domain-Driven Design exploration of the Educational Domain,
- a structured language for software architects, analysts, developers, educators and domain experts,
- a contribution to Human–AI collaboration in knowledge work.

## What this work is not

This manuscript is not:

- a commercial product,
- a regulatory framework,
- a universal educational standard,
- a formal academic textbook,
- a substitute for institutional, legal or educational judgment.
