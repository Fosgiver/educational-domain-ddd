## Overview

This repository hosts the first public version of **Educational Domain — Domain-Driven Design Manuscript**.

The work is a freely available architectural manuscript exploring the Educational Domain through Domain-Driven Design, systems analysis, educational experience, and Human–AI collaboration.

Its purpose is to help software architects, analysts, developers, educators, and domain experts reason about educational organizations before designing the information systems that support them.

## Origin of the Work

This manuscript was not written as a literature review and does not rely on a formal bibliography.

Its concepts, terminology, distinctions, and architectural proposals emerged through an extended dialogue between Konstantinos Andritsopoulos’ professional experience and Artificial Intelligence.

The work was shaped through discussion, questioning, refinement, correction, and step-by-step architectural reasoning.

## Human–AI Collaboration

This project is also a practical example of how meaningful work can emerge from the cooperation between human experience and Artificial Intelligence.

The human contribution brought decades of experience in education, software development, workflow systems, teaching, organizational management, and the practical reality of educational institutions.

The AI contribution supported structure, synthesis, questioning, consistency checking, linguistic refinement, and architectural organization.

Neither replaced the other.  
The value emerged from collaboration.

## Guiding Principle

> Technology should adapt to educational reality.  
> Educational reality should never be forced to adapt to technology.
